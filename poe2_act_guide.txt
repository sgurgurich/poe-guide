## Act 1
    1. Riverbank
    2. Town
    3. Clearfell
        - Kill Witch for 10% Flat Cold Res
        - Find and kill worm if you need skill gem
    4. The Grelwood
        - Find waypoint at big tree
        - Find The Grim Tangle and enter to get waypoint
        - Find witch in hut and kill for 2 medium flasks
    5. The Red Vale
        - Find 3 obelisks, kill boss
    6. Go back to town
        - Talk to Renly to get item
    7. The Grelwood
        - Take item back to The Grelwood tree and use to free Sin
        - Talk to Una at tree
    8. Go back to town
        - Talk to Una
    9. The Grim Tangle
        - Summon Una to break vines and proceed
        - Kill miniboss if you need gem
    10. Cemetary of the Eternals
        - Find Mausoleum of the Praetor
        - Find Mausoleum of the Consort
        - Find grave event for a free ring
    11. Clear bosses in the Mausoleums
    12. Cemetary of the Eternals
        - Talk to guy at waypoint
        - Kill boss
        - Go back to town after to awaken Sin for auto Identifying items and Respeccing
    13. Hunting Grounds
        - Find the Crowbell boss for 2 skill points
        - Find Ogham Farmlands zone, enter and get waypoint for later
        - Find Freythorn zone
    14. Freythorn 
        - Find and clear rituals
        - Kill King in the Mists for 30 spirit and spirit gem
        - If you get to final alter before finding all the small ones, it will emit black wisps pointing to the
          missing alters
    15. Ogham Farmlands
        - Find hut with Una's Lutebox for 2 passive points (turn in at town)
        - Find crop circle event for free gem
    16. Ogham Village
        - Find Renly's Blacksmithing tools to unlock salvaging
        - Kill hangman boss
        - CLICK THE LADY IN THE CAGE AT THE END
    17. The Manor Ramparts
        - Find the hanging body for free support gem
    18. Ogham Manor
        - Scariest zone in the act, be careful
        - On the floor you enter into, find the Candle boss for 20 max health
        - Kill the Act boss at the end!

## Act 3
    1. Sandswept Marsh
        - Miniboss is super optional
        - Find camp with chest if you need a free Lesser Jeweller's orb
    2. Town
    3. Jungle Ruins
        - Kill monkey for 2 passive points
        - Find waypoint and The Venom Crypts
        - Find Infested Barrens
    4. Infested Barrens
        - Find waypoint and travel to The Venom Crypts via Jungle Ruins waypoint
    5. The Venom Crypts
        - Find the body with the venom phial
        - Bring back to the lady in town
        - Choose a reward (usually mana regen or ailment threshold)
    6. Infested Barrens
        - Find the Azak Bog
    7. Azak Bog
        - Find and kill witch boss for 30 Spirit and spirit gem
    8. Infested Barrens
        - Find Chimeral Wetlands
    9. Chimeral Wetlands
        - Kill plant rare if you need a free amulet
        - Find entrance to Temple of Chaos for later, get waypoint
        - Find Chimera boss and kill it, pick up the free ultimatum key
        - Enter Jiquani's Machinarium at the BACK of the boss arena
    10. Jiquani's Machinarium
        - Find soul core to proceed through first gate
        - Find 2 sould cores. DO NOT USE ONE ON THE TREASURE ROOM
        - Use 1 for the entrance to Jiquani's Sanctum
        - Use the other for the hidden boss, kill him for 10% fire res
    11. Jiquani's Sanctum
        - Go left side, find medium soul core, place in left generator
        - Waypoint back to beginning
        - Go right side, find medium soul core, place in right generator
        - RUN BACK TO THE BEGINNING (you have to follow the red light on the ground)
        - Kill the boss and take the large soul core it drops
    12. Infested Barrens
        - Place large soul core in pedestal next to waypoint
    13. The Matlan Waterways
        - Go slow, click levers to move forward.
        - Pull big lever at the end and portal to town
    14. The Drowned City
        - Go Southeast in town and down the big stairs behind Sin
        - Watch out for the water witches and the Drowned Orbs
        - Find entrance to the Molten Vault
    15. The Molten Vault
        - Get to the end, kill boss for Reforging Bench
    16. The Drowned City
        - Find entrance for The Apex of Filth
    17. The Apex of Filth
        - Ignore mushrooms that drop
        - Kill boss at the end and pick up golden idol
    18. Town
        - Bring golden idol to temple door at the bottom of the big stairs
    19. The Temple of Kopec
        - Stay in the shade
        - Stairs to move forward will always be in the corners of the Triangle
        - Get to top and kill the boss
        - Talk to Alva in the boss room
    20. Utzaal
        - Go through the time portal and down the stairs
        - Proceed through town
        - Kill as much as you can, you want to find a Sacrifical Heart for a skill point later
        - Kill Viper boss (good luck)
        - Proceed through town to the Aggorat entrance
    21. Aggorat
        - Keep looking for Sacrifical Heart if you still need it
        - Proceed through zone, when it gets bloody, start looking for the Sacrifical Alter
        - Place heart on alter, stab it with the knife they give you and you get 2 skill points
        - Find the entrance to The Black Chambers
    22. The Black Chambers
        - Find and 'kill' Doryani
